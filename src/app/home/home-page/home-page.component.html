Instructions

  1. Behind one of three presented doors is a car, and behind the other two are goats.
  2. Select a door.
  3. The host will reveal a goat behind one of the unchosen doors.
  4. You'll be given the option to either stay with your original choice or switch to the other unopened door.
  5. Make your final decision.
  6. The doors will open, and you'll see if you've won the car!
