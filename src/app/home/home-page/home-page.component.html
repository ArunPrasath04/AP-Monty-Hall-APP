Welcome Player!!!!

Instructions

  1. Enter the desired number of simulations.
  2. Decide whether to always switch doors or always stay with your initial choice.
  3. The game involves one car and two goats hidden behind three doors.
  4. In each simulation:
    • You'll first pick a door.
    • The host will then reveal a goat behind a different, unopened door.
    • You'll have the option to switch your choice to the remaining closed door.
    • Make your final selection.
    • The doors will open, revealing if you've won the car!
  5. Finally the application reveals how many times you have won an lost the car.
